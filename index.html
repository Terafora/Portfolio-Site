Want To Know A Little More About Me?

I'm a full-stack developer who after lot's of self-study is currently undergoing a developer bootcamp with Code Institute. The purpose of the bootcamp is to broaden my experience with web technologies such as JavaScript, Python, Django etc. as well learn development methodologies as well as get some valuable experience working in a development team for the hackathons.

I began my tech journey back in 2011 on an apprenticeship in East London. Through hard work I was able to leave this apprenticeship with a software engineer position secured at Capgemini where I stayed for the better part of 5 years working alongside a diverse group of colleagues in Central London supporting remote users and helping setup and maintain SQL servers.

I took a break after this to finish up my university studies and try my hand at another passion of mine, and so I moved to rural Japan to teach English language and foreign culture. Although I loved this work I knew that I still yearned to work in tech. so instead of going back to maintaining others' work and fixing issues I decided to once again try my hand at something different and began working towards becoming a web developer.

Some other little bits about me:

  • BA in Japanese Language & Film and Media (Dual-Honours)
  • Avid learner
  • I speak English and Japanese (as well as some French and Spanish)
  • Current learning self-studying React.JS
